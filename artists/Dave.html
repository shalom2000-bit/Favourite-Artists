UK's Brightest Star

Dave has gained acclaim for his socially conscious lyricism and wordplay, and is among the most recognised British rappers. Dave released his debut extended play Six Paths in 2016, after the release of a number of successful singles including the grime song "Thiago Silva" with AJ Tracey.
